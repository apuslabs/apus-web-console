# Setup Wallet

Setting up your Ethereum wallet is a breeze:

1. **Install MetaMask**: [Download here](https://metamask.io/download.html)
2. **Create a Wallet**: [Creation guide](https://support.metamask.io/hc/en-us/articles/4404722782107)
3. **Connect to Networks**:
    <Steps
        className="my-2"
        direction="vertical"
        current={-1}
          items={[
              {
                  title: 'Add chains to your wallet',
                  description: <div>
                      <Button type="primary" onClick={switchSepolia} className="mb-2">Connect to Sepolia</Button>
                      <br />
                      <Button type="primary" onClick={switchTaiko}>Connect to Taiko</Button>
                  </div>,
              },
              {
                  title: 'Add tokens to your wallet',
                  description: <div>
                      <Button type="primary" onClick={addAPEToken}>Add APE Token</Button>
                  </div>,
              },
          ]}
        />
4. **Get Sepolia Test Tokens**
    You can receive Sepolia ETH from several online faucets. Here are a few you can try:
    - [sepolia-faucet.pk910.de](https://sepolia-faucet.pk910.de/)
    - [faucet-sepolia.rockx.com](https://faucet-sepolia.rockx.com/)
    - [sepoliafaucet.com](https://sepoliafaucet.com/)
    - [infura.io](https://www.infura.io/faucet/sepolia)
    - [quicknode.com](https://faucet.quicknode.com/ethereum/sepolia)
5. **Bridge Tokens to Taiko**: [Bridge here](https://taiko.xyz/docs/guides/bridge-tokens)

> **Note**: 0.01 ETH is needed to cover Gas fees on the Ethereum network. Keep your private keys secure to prevent scams and theft.
